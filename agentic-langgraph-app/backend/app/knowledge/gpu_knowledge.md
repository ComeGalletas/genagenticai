## Product: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5090

**Category:** GPU  
**Manufacturer:** NVIDIA  
**Aliases:** RTX 5090, GeForce RTX 5090

### Overview

The NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5090 is the flagship consumer graphics card of the RTX 50 Series, based on the Blackwell architecture. It is designed for enthusiast gaming, AI workloads, content creation, and professional applications requiring maximum GPU performance.

### Specifications

- Architecture: Blackwell
- VRAM: 32 GB GDDR7
- Memory Bus: 512-bit
- CUDA Cores: 21760
- TDP: 575 W
- PCI Express: PCIe 5.0

### Features

- DLSS 4
- Multi Frame Generation
- Fourth-generation Ray Tracing acceleration
- Fifth-generation Tensor Cores
- DisplayPort 2.1
- AV1 hardware encoding and decoding

### Performance

The RTX 5090 targets native 4K and 8K gaming, professional rendering, AI inference, and large generative AI models. It represents the highest level of gaming performance in the RTX 50 Series.

### Pricing

Launch MSRP: $1,999 USD
